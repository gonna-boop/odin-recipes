Rum Ham Recipe

Description

Hello, welcome to my first website! Here you will be able to learn all the necessary steps to make one of the finest dishes known to man, the famous Rum Hum.

ingredients

  • 10lb smoked ham fully-cooked, bone-in
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of dark brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp of dijon mustard
  • 1/3 cup dark rum

Steps

  1. Score the ham in criss-cross patterns then wrap it in foil and bake it for 2 hours or so.
  2. Just before the 2-hour mark, whisk up the brown sugar, rum, and mustard.
  3. Then it's time to grab your pastry brush. Generously brush the ham with the rum glaze then bake it a little longer in the oven. This will caramelize the sugars in the glaze, giving the ham a beautiful color and enhancing its flavor.
